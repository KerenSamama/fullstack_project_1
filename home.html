Horses Boarding

The boarding house is intended for people who have a horse but have nowhere to put it and no way to take care of it. We offer our beautiful and spacious cabins for your horses. If you want your horse to be taken care of wholeheartedly, contact us.

Therapeutic Riding

Therapeutic riding is intended for populatio with special needs. This ride is designed to help with a variety of situations and problems - starting with mental problems and ending with various physical and physical limitations. Therapeutic horseback riding has been proven to be effective in the treatment of attention disorders, hyperactivity, post-traumatic conditions, insecurity, autism, and more.

Sporty Riding

The green farm offers horse riding lessons in the English - sporty riding style. The riding lessons we offer are a practical and theoretical course. The duration of each lesson is half an hour. The riding lessons are personal and delivered by experienced riding instructors. More experienced and advanced riders can participate in national competitions.
